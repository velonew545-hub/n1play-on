Seasonal Events Overview

Community markets, clean-ups, and educational talks occur year-round.

  • New Year's Eve: Celebrate with sustainable traditions and winter events on Dec 31st.
  • Fair Trade Month: Campaigns promoting ethical trade, usually in October.
  • Earth Day: Nationwide environmental activities in April.

FAQs

Are events free?

Many are free or low-cost; some require tickets.

Suitable for families?

Often yes. Check event details for age guidance.

Do I need to bring anything?

Water, sun protection, and a small bag for reusables help.
