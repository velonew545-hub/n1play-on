New York City: The Big Apple

A global hub of culture, finance, and dining. From the iconic Statue of Liberty to the vibrant streets of Brooklyn, NYC is a city that never sleeps.

  • Must-See: Central Park, The Met Museum, and the Empire State Building.
  • Tip: Walk the High Line for a unique urban park experience.
  • Transport: The Subway is the fastest way to get around.

San Francisco: The Golden City

Famous for the Golden Gate Bridge, fog, and tech innovation. A hilly city with stunning bay views.

  • Highlights: Alcatraz Island, Fisherman's Wharf, and Golden Gate Park.
  • Culture: Ride the historic cable cars.

Chicago: The Windy City

Known for its bold architecture and beautiful Lake Michigan shoreline.

  • Attractions: Millennium Park (The Bean) and the Art Institute.
  • Vibe: Take an architecture river cruise.

Austin: Live Music Capital

A vibrant city in Texas known for its eclectic live music scene and outdoor spaces.

  • Landmark: The Texas State Capitol and Barton Springs Pool.
  • Event: SXSW Festival in March.

Seattle: The Emerald City

Surrounded by water, mountains, and evergreen forests, it's the home of grunge music and coffee culture.

  • Visit: Pike Place Market and the Space Needle.
  • Nature: Take a ferry to nearby islands.

Washington D.C.: The Capital

The political heart of the nation, filled with iconic monuments and free museums.

  • Sights: The National Mall, Lincoln Memorial, and Smithsonian Museums.
  • Green Tip: Use the Capital Bikeshare system.

Portland: Rose City

Known for its parks, bridges, and bicycle paths, as well as its eco-friendliness.

  • Culture: Explore Powell's City of Books.
  • Nature: Visit the International Rose Test Garden.

FAQs

What is the best time to visit New York?

Spring (April-June) and Fall (September-November) offer the most pleasant weather.

Is public transport good in US cities?

Major cities like NYC, Chicago, and DC have extensive systems, but renting a car is often best for other regions.

Do I need cash?

Credit cards are accepted almost everywhere, but keeping some cash for small tips is useful.
